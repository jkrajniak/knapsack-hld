## Interpretation

- mean gap at α=0.5 — **+0.0014%** (vs α=0.0: +0.0025%, α=1.0: +0.0028%)
- α=0.5 vs endpoint mean: -0.0012% (dip).
- worst α: **0.1** (+0.0028%); best α: **0.2** (+0.0009%).
- mean cv(B_k) at α=0.0/0.5/1.0 = 0.000 / 0.007 / 0.015 — linear in α as expected (Phase-2 design).
- **Verdict: NO MECHANISTIC SPIKE.** The α=0.5 row is indistinguishable from the endpoints (|Δ| < 5e-5 = 0.005 %). The Fig 5 visual wobble is dominated by the HiGHS-tolerance artefact already addressed in §4.3.5, not by a Phase-2-induced sub-MILP regime change.
